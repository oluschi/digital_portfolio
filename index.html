Museum Design

Type Design 2021.

For this project we were tasked with developing branding for a museum concept of our choice. The project consists of creating a logo, 2 posters; a main museum and an event poster, and a website mockup. I choose to make a museum that commemorates some of London’s icons fashion designers that emerged from King’s Road.


GENZARCHIVES.COM

Website Design + Development, 2021.

GENZARCHIVES.COM will take you down memory lane and capture the essences of the childhood of Generation Z. This site provides users with an archive of movies, games, tv shows, and other miscellaneous items that defined our childhood.

For this project we were tasked with developing a website through the use of Api systems and Javascript. The site had to provide a filtering system for the content and have a consistent theme throughout. My site inspiration came from late 2000s to early 2010s styles of game sites, archiving information from generation z childhood all in one site.

Trend Forecasting: Mimi Wade

Digital Collage, 2021.

For my Fashion Trend Analysis class we were tasked with developing trend boards form a company of our choice using information from trend forecasting sites such as WGSN and Stylus.The project focused on creating boards based on the upcoming trends for season of Spring’22, that incorporates the overall aesthetic of the brand. For my project, I choose to do the designer Mimi Wade and her self-named brand. Through my research I was able to develop 4 future forecast boards that may be utilized for this brand.


Event Site: Afro Futurism In Fashion

Website Design + Development, 2020.

Afro Futurism in Fashion is an event that promotes the voices of black designers who create with this narrative in mind. Through the use of web design, I wanted to showcase the beauty of the black diaspora of Afro Futurism through the use of vibrant colors, intricate design, geometric shapes, and overall unique expression through design.


Manifesto Posters: Taking Up Space

Type Design 2020.

This project focused on the creation of a series of posters based on a manifesto of our choice. For my design, I chose the manifesto “Taking Up Space” by Chelsea Kwaky and Ore Ogunbiyi. This manifesto discusses the lack of diversity in higher education, and addresses the topic such as decolonising curriculum, access, mental health, and activism within this setting.


WikiBook: 2010s in Fashion

Type Design + Collage, 2020.

For this project, we were tasked to create a book based on a wikipedia page of our choice. The topic I chose was “2010s in Fashion”, which explores and defines the styles trends throughout the decade. The inspiration behind the design of my book is heavily inspired by the nostalgic essence of early y2k handwritten. At the end I created a 105 page book and a website mockup.


Dynamic Word + Mantra Merch

Type Design + Gifs, 2020.

For this project, I chose the mantra “Smile For Me”. inspired by the hit 2000s song “Grillz” by Nelly. From this mantra I went on to create merchandise and gifts. The gifs were made using Adobe AfterEffects in order to create the rotation and 3D effect to my design.
